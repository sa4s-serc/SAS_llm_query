The Qutub Minar is a famous monument located near the Qutub Shahi Tombs in Hyderabad, India. It was built by the Qutub Shah, who is also known for his contributions to modern Indian architecture. On the other hand, the Golconda Fort is a major fortress located in the heart of Hyderabad. It was constructed by Rao Hari Singh in 1608 and was named after the Golconda River. The fortress is home to several temples and mosques, including the Siddhartha Peetha and the Mughal Garden. Visitors can explore the fortress and its surroundings, including the Kacheerla Gulabi, a beautiful garden filled with flowers.
