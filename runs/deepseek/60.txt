Its stunning Indo-Islamic architecture features four grand arches and intricate designs, symbolizing the city's rich heritage. As you explore the area, make sure to visit the nearby Mecca Masjid, one of the largest mosques in India, known for its beautiful architecture and serene atmosphere.
