The structure features four grand arches and is adorned with beautiful minarets that rise to a height of 56 meters. Besides its architectural beauty, Charminar is surrounded by vibrant markets, making it a perfect spot to immerse yourself in the local culture.

**Qutb Shahi Tombs:** Located about 3 kilometers from Charminar, the Qutb Shahi Tombs are a series of magnificent tombs built for the rulers of the Qutb Shahi dynasty. Dating back to the 16th century, these tombs showcase stunning Indo-Islamic architecture and beautifully landscaped gardens. It’s a serene place to learn about the history of the Golconda Sultanate.

**Complementary Activity:** After visiting the tombs, you could explore **Golconda Fort**, which is nearby. This grand fortress is famous for its acoustics and stunning views of the city. The history and stories surrounding the fort will surely fascinate you.
